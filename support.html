Support

How to get help from the Midwest office.

Use this page for estimate follow-up, existing service questions, scheduling concerns, and general contact information for Midwest Equipment Dock and Lift Services.

For new estimate requests

The fastest path is still the estimate form on the homepage. Include the lake name, property address, your best phone number, and details about the dock, lift, shoreline, or storage work you need.

For existing customers

If you already work with Midwest and need an update on repair, seasonal service, winterization, or storage, call the office directly so the team can route your question quickly.

Photos help

When you reach out, include current photos of the shoreline, dock, lift, and any damage or access issue. That helps the crew understand your setup before the next call or visit.
