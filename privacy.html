Privacy

How Midwest handles website information.

This page explains what information the Midwest website collects through estimate requests and page-view reporting, and how that information is used by the office team.

Information you submit

When you fill out the estimate form, Midwest receives the contact and project information you provide, such as your name, phone number, email address, lake, property address, timing, and project details.

How that information is used

Midwest uses estimate-request information to review your project, contact you about service, and coordinate sales, repair, installation, removal, storage, winterization, or shoreline support.

Basic page reporting

The website also sends limited page-view information to the Midwest admin app, such as page path, page title, referrer, and session identifier. This helps the company understand which pages are being used most often.

No sale of personal data

Midwest does not use this website to sell personal information. Information submitted through the site is used to respond to service and sales inquiries and to improve the website.

Questions About Privacy?

If you have questions about information submitted through the website, contact the Midwest office directly.
