Questions We Hear Often

Straight answers before you commit to the work.

Do you only work on certain lakes?

Yes. Midwest serves lake properties throughout Northeast Wisconsin, including Legend Lake, White Potato Lake, Lake Noquebay, Chute Pond, Shawano Lake, High Falls Flowage, Boulder Lake, Maiden Lake, Kelly Lake, and many more throughout the region.

Can you help if my current setup no longer fits my boat?

Yes. Reconfiguration, adjustment, and repair work are a big part of what makes Midwest valuable for both longtime customers and new customers who need more than a basic install.

What helps you give a faster estimate?

Lake name, property address, shoreline photos, and a few notes about the dock, lift, or problem you are dealing with.

Do you help with storm damage or shoreline issues?

Yes. If the problem affects your access, shoreline, dock, or lift, Midwest can help assess the issue and recommend the right next step.
